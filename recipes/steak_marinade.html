Steak Tip Marinade

Description

This steak tip recipe uses a mouth-watering marinade made with Worcestershire sauce, Italian-style salad dressing, garlic pepper seasoning, and barbeque sauce. It took many experiments to finally perfect this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Â½ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 cup Italian-style salad dressing
  • 2 teaspoons garlic pepper seasoning
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce
  • 2 pounds beef sirloin tip steaks

How to make Steak Tip Marinade

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Mix Worcestershire sauce, Italian-style salad dressing, garlic pepper seasoning, and barbeque sauce in a medium bowl.
  3. Step 3: Place steaks in marinade and turn to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  4. Step 4: Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate. Place steaks on preheated grill; discard marinade. Cook steaks until they are beginning to firm and are hot and slightly pink in the center, about 10 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 140 degrees F (60 degrees C) for medium.
